Summarization Engine

Summarize any text with a click of a button

Within a minute, our summarizer can reduce articles, news data, or documents down to the most important points. Our Trained model employs natural language processing (NLP) to locate crucial information while preserving the original context. The system also gives a knowledge graph and frequency-based keywords. We also have a system in place to assess previously summarised paragraphs.

Key Features


Abstractive Summary


Abstractive Text Summarization is the task of generating a short and concise summary that captures the salient ideas of the source text. The generated summaries potentially contain new phrases and sentences that may not appear in the source text.


Result Analysis


There are various analysis tools available like rouge score, BLEU score, which are only concerned with the structural evaluation of the summaries. Project mainly concerned with the semantic evaluation which analysis the summary at a deeper level rather than on the structural level.


Knowledge graph


Knowledge Graph is also integrated from the summary extracted. A knowledge graph, also known as a semantic network, represents a network of real-world entities i.e. objects, events, or concepts and illustrates the relationship between them. Also, the most frequent nouns are obtained with NLP Corpus
